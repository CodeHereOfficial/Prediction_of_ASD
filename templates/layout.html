Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is the name for a group of developmental disorders impacting the nervous system. ASD symptoms range from mild to severe: mainly language impairment, challenges in social interaction, and repetitive behaviors. Many other possible symptoms include anxiety, mood disorders and Attention- Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD).
